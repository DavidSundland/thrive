David Sundland

Recruitee

A problem-solver with a creative bent. David has returned to his engineering roots, after his passion for urban design took him from Arizona to Illinois and Virginia as a city planner. His love of dogs prompted a second career as a self-employed dog trainer. Then his affinity for live music and frustration with the lack of good online resources for finding concerts guided him to his true calling - as a web developer.

David's coding career was launched with the creation of District Concerts, DC's leading music calendar. While the site's revenue generation never met expectations, the experience of teaching himself HTML, CSS, Python, and WordPress in order to build the site inspired him to commit to an immersive program studying the full stack. He is now ready to bring his enthusiasm to Viget as a Front-End Developer Apprentice.
